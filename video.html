Time together is a fun and collaborative calendar app.

Keep track of your daily, weekly, monthly and yearly schedules through an online interface that is accessible on your phone, tablet and pc. Manage your tasks easier with a clean visual calendar, customize events with colors and emojis.

You have a new feature that allows users access to calendar rooms which is a shared calender for your friends and family.

In each calendar room, members can be assigned into events and events are visible to only people within the group.

Need to chat? No problem!

Time together has a chat service where you can chat with friends and family. No matter the day or time, Time Together can be use any time of the day and you can spend time together with friends and family on this new calendar app!
